I am currently a tenure-track assistant professor at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, working with Prof. Ping Zhang and Prof. Guangyu Wang. I received the Ph.D. degree from Tianjin University under the supervision of Prof. [Changqing Zhang](https://cic.tju.edu.cn/faculty/zhangchangqing/research.html), who is the best mentor I could ask for, always providing valuable guidance and support. My research interests are on multimodal machine learning and their applications on smart medicine. I have published more than 20 papers in top international AI conferences and journals, including IEEE TPAMI, NeurIPS, ICLR, and CVPR, with a total of <a href='https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=F2BBkQEAAAAJ'><img src="https://img.shields.io/endpoint?url={{ url | url_encode }}&logo=Google%20Scholar&labelColor=f6f6f6&color=9cf&style=flat&label=citations"></a> citations on Google Scholar.
